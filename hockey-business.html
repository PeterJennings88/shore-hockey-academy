A Unique Perspective on Hockey

Drew Shore isn't just a former NHL player — he's one of the top hockey agents in the industry, with deep expertise in contract negotiations, career management, and the business side of professional hockey.

After his playing career, Drew transitioned into player representation, where he quickly established himself as a trusted advisor for professional hockey players. His firsthand experience in the NHL, combined with his business acumen, gives him a unique ability to guide players through every aspect of their careers.

At Shore Hockey Academy, Drew shares this invaluable knowledge with players and families who want to understand what it really takes to build a successful career in hockey — from youth hockey all the way to the pros.

The Agent's Perspective

"Playing in the NHL taught me the game. Being an agent taught me the business. Most players and families have no idea how the hockey industry really works until they're already in it. I want to change that." — Drew Shore

What Most Players Don't Know

The path to professional hockey is filled with decisions that can make or break a career. Which junior league is right? When should you go to college vs. turn pro? How do contract negotiations really work? Drew has seen it all from both sides — as a player and as the person negotiating the deals.

Why This Matters Early

By the time most families think about the business side of hockey, critical decisions have already been made. Understanding the landscape early — even at the youth hockey level — helps families make informed choices that set players up for long-term success.
