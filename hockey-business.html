Drew Shore is a former NHL player and a trusted advisor with deep experience in career management, recruiting strategy, and the business side of hockey.

After his playing career, Drew transitioned into player representation and advisory services. His firsthand experience in elite hockey combined with business insight gives families a practical edge in high-stakes career decisions.

At Shore Hockey Academy, Drew shares this knowledge with players and families looking to understand what it really takes to build a long-term path in hockey.
