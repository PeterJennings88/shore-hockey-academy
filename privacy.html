Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 11, 2026

What We Collect

When you submit camp or consultation forms, we collect information such as player name, age, parent or guardian contact details, interest area, and message details you provide.

How We Use Information

We use submitted data to respond to inquiries, evaluate camp fit, provide program information, and coordinate enrollment or consultation scheduling.

Youth Data and Parent Consent

Our services involve minors. Form submissions must be completed by a parent or legal guardian. By submitting, you confirm you are authorized to provide information for the player.

Data Storage and Processors

Lead submissions are processed through Airtable for CRM storage and email notification providers for operational follow-up.

Analytics

We use analytics tools such as Google Analytics 4 to understand website performance and improve conversion experience. Analytics data may include page views, events, and campaign parameters.

Retention

We retain lead information only as long as needed for business and operational purposes, unless a longer period is required by law.

Your Choices

To request updates or deletion of submitted information, contact us at info@shorehockeyacademy.com.
