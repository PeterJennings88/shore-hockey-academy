Train Like the Pros

Shore Hockey Academy's Elite Summer Camp is the most exclusive youth hockey program in the country. Limited to just 24 AAA-level players per session, this is not your average hockey camp.

Application Required: Every player must apply and be vetted by our coaching staff. We review game footage, coach recommendations, and player statistics to ensure each participant is competing at a AAA level or higher. This rigorous selection process ensures an elite training environment where top players push each other to improve.

Our intensive week-long sessions focus on every aspect of the game: skating technique, stick handling, shooting accuracy, defensive positioning, and game strategy at the highest level.
