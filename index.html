About Drew Shore

From Denver to the NHL

Drew Shore's hockey journey began at the University of Denver, where he developed into one of the top collegiate players in the nation. His exceptional performance earned him a spot in the NHL, where he competed at the highest level of professional hockey.

During his NHL career, Drew played for multiple teams including the Florida Panthers, Calgary Flames, and Vancouver Canucks. His professional experience spans years of elite competition, giving him invaluable insights into what it takes to succeed at every level of hockey.

Now, Drew brings his wealth of knowledge and passion for the game back to Colorado, dedicated to developing the next generation of hockey players through Shore Hockey Academy.
